Homemade Pav Bhaji is the best for a Sunday!  Added with extra butter and  some lemon...It totally makes my day !
Ingredients:-
1) Potatoes - 2
2) Cauliflower - half
3) Carrot - half 
4) Chopped Onion and Capsicum - according to ur taste
5) Chopped Tomatoes - 3
6) Grated garlic - 10
7) Green Peas 
8) Pav Bhaji Masala , Salt to taste , Kashmiri red chilli powder - All 2-3 tsp.
. :Steps and Procedure :-
1. Boil the veggies in a pressure cooker for 2 whistles on medium flame and keep the water left after the boiling in separate container. 
2. Mash all the veggies. Now heat a pan and add some butter n oil to it. Then add green chilli , ginger,garlic and fry it for a minute on medium flame.

3. Then add onions to the mixture and fry till soft and turning yellow. Stir nicely.

4. Add Chopped Capsicum and green peas . Mix it and fry for again a min after which u can add the tomatoes and stir it carefully.

5. Add some salt to taste and fry for 3-4 mins . After this , add kashmiri red chilli powder and pavbhaji masala to it .

6. Cover the pan and let it simmer for 30 - 40 sec so that all masalas get roasted well . At this point , add mashed veggies and mix well. 
KEEP MASHING THE BHAJI FOR THAT RICH FLAVOUR 
7. Now add some water which u kept separately and stir for sometime. Again add very less salt. 
8. Now  stir the mixture and cover the pan to let it simmer for 2-3 mins. It's Ready!........Add some butter and coriander leaves for the extra delicious flavour.
Don't Forget to add lemon too!! 9. Toast the pavs with butter and SERVE HOT with some chopped onions!! Thank me later ğŸ˜‚
